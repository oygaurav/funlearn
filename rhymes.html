Rhymes

Welcome to our Rhymes Fun Zone, where learning is a joyful song! Let's dive into colorful rhymes and melodies that'll make learning fun and exciting!

12345

It's a great way to help children learn to count and associate numbers with their corresponding fingers
ABC Song

Get ready to sing and learn the alphabet with our colorful characters while getting familiar with letters and their sounds.
Baba Baba Black Sheep

It's a playful way to for kids to learn language sounds and patterns while enjoying the imagery of a black sheep with bags of wool.
Boo Boo

Kids will learn about empathy and kindness while enjoying the catchy melody.

Five Little Monkey

Join the mischievous five little monkeys in their hilarious escapades. These monkeys bring laughter and valuable lessons.
Happy Birthday

The "Happy Birthday" song brings people together to celebrate and make the birthday person feel special on their special day.
Hickery Dickery

The whimsical tale of a mouse running up and down a clock that helps kids develop a sense of rhythm and rhyme.
Humpty Dumpty

The tale of Humpty’s fall from a wall and the failed attempts to mend him creates a mix of humor and a sense of caution. 
Itsy Bitsy Spider

Rhyme that follows the journey of a determined little spider as it encounters a series of obstacles.
Jack and Jill

"Jack and Jill" is often used to teach basic concepts of rhyme and rhythm while also engaging children with its story.
Jonny Jonny

A popular nursery rhyme that portrays a playful interaction between a child named Johnny and his father. 
Little Miss Muffet

The tale of Little Miss Muffet being startled by a spider while enjoying her meal has a touch of humor and surprise.
Little Red Riding Hood

Step into the enchanting world of Little Red Riding Hood as she embarks on a journey through the woods.
Old McDonald

The song continues with additional verses, each introducing a new animal and its associated sound.
Row Row Row

The song is often sung in a round, where different groups start singing at different times to create a harmonious effect.

Story Time

Join our characters as they dive into books and embark on adventures through storytelling.

The Little Kittens

This rhyme teaches kids about responsibility, consequences, and the importance of taking care of their belongings.
There Little Pigs

Join the three little pigs on their quest to outsmart the big bad wolf! Kids learn the value of hard work and clever thinking.
Two Little Dickie Bird

"Two Little Dickie Birds" is a charming nursery rhyme that involves finger movements and is often enjoyed by young children.
Wash Your Hands

Kids are reminded of the importance of proper handwashing. They see characters demonstrate the steps, making it fun.
